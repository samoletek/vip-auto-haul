Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I schedule vehicle transport?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for standard service and 3-4 weeks for enclosed transport during peak seasons. For expedited service, we can often accommodate requests with 3-5 days' notice, depending on route and availability.

What insurance coverage is provided during transport?

All vehicles transported by VIP Auto Haul are fully insured with comprehensive coverage during the entire transport process. We provide detailed documentation of our coverage before transport begins, and our policy covers any potential damage that might occur during loading, transit, and unloading.

How should I prepare my vehicle for transport?

To prepare your vehicle, we recommend: maintaining a quarter tank of fuel, removing personal items, disabling alarms, retracting antennas, folding mirrors (if possible), and documenting any pre-existing damage. Our transport specialist will provide a detailed preparation checklist specific to your vehicle type prior to pickup.

Can I track my vehicle during transport?

Yes, all VIP Auto Haul transports include real-time GPS tracking. You'll receive access to our client portal where you can check your vehicle's location. Additionally, your dedicated transport coordinator will provide regular updates throughout the journey and immediately notify you of any schedule changes.
