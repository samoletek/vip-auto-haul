Frequently Asked Questions.

How long does auto transport typically take?

Transport times vary based on distance, ranging from 1-2 days for short distances (under 500 miles) to 7-10 days for coast-to-coast shipments. Our team will provide a specific estimate for your route during the quote process.

What type of insurance coverage do you provide?

All vehicles transported by VIP Auto Haul are covered by our comprehensive cargo insurance policy which includes protection against damage during loading, transport, and unloading. We provide full documentation of coverage before transport begins.

How do I prepare my vehicle for transport?

We recommend removing personal belongings, leaving only 1/4 tank of fuel, disabling alarms, documenting any existing damage, and providing a set of keys. Our transport specialist will provide a detailed preparation checklist specific to your vehicle.
